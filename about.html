About Me

How I Got Started

I’ve always been really interested in how technology works. Over time, that interest turned into a passion for keeping it safe. I wanted to understand what’s going on inside computers and networks, so I decided to focus on cybersecurity. To get started, I went to Perak Matriculation College for my pre-university studies. That’s where I built a strong basic knowledge of the field.

How I Built My Foundation

After high school, I chose to study Computer Network Security. That helped me learn the basics of hardware, how to build strong network systems, and how to defend them.

How I Became a Builder

I don’t just want to use technology—I want to know how it really works and how to protect it. That curiosity pushed me to learn more about network setups, containerization, and identity management. I enjoy building safe and strong systems from scratch. For example, I designed and set up a password-free Zero Trust login system, as well as an encrypted private cloud storage system.

How I Embrace Challenges

I do my best work under pressure—when quick thinking and fast problem-solving really matter. Whether I'm looking through network logs in Wireshark to find and fix weak spots, racing against the clock in cybersecurity hackathons, or handling big projects with tight deadlines, I see every challenge as a chance to get better at what I do. It all helps me grow into the kind of security professional I want to become.
